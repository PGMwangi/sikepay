FAQs

1. What is blockchain technology?

Blockchain is a digital ledger technology that allows transactions to be recorded and verified in a secure and transparent way. Each block in the chain contains a record of multiple transactions and is linked to the previous block, creating a chain of blocks that cannot be altered.
